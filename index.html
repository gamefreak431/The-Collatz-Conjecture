The Collatz Conjecture

Also known as 3x+1

The rules:

  • Start with any positive number.
  • Then determine if the number is odd or even.
  • If the number is even: Divide it by two.
  • If the number is odd, multiply by three and add one.

The conjecture is that no matter what number you pick, the sequence will always eventually reach 1.
